Bio

Ryan Yuzuki has been programming ever since his parents installed BlueJ on the family computer. Ever heard of Rancho Palos Verdes? No? It's this city in the south of Los Angeles County where Ryan grew up, known for its seaside bluffs and tranquil atmosphere. Ryan recieved his BS in Computer Science from the University of California, Riverside and currently lives between the LA/OC area. In additon to programming, he loves video games, film, and logic puzzles! Also, he wrote this in the third person, and it felt ...weird.

Career

I am a software engineer, working with Raytheon. I started there as an intern in July 2018, and resumed full time in July 2019, after graduation.

Film

Movies [Film, Cinema, Kino] has been one of my favorite pastimes for as long as I can remember. My favorite genres include Slice of Life, Coming of Age and Psychological Thriller. My favorite film at the moment would be The Florida Project, by Sean Baker. I love the way it portrays something never really seen in cinema, and the struggles of the underrpresented.

Television

I do enjoy Television shows, though not to the extent of film. I generally feel Television requires less focus, due to an established universe. Drama more for direct watching, and Comedy more for putting on in the background while doing something else. My favorite television show is Master of None. Honorable mention to Black Mirror.
