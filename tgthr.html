Who are we?

We are an overambicious group of computer science students currently attending the University of California Riverside. We have decided to create TGTHR as a project for our CS180 Software Engineering class. We were inspired by applications such as Meetup and Eventbrite. In building TGTHR, we have utilized mostly React Native, with Firebase as our database. In the future, keep your eyes on this place for some majorly planned updates!
